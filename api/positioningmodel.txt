a corner is one of the 4 real corners or the middle of one of the 4 edges of a hud.

a hud may or may not be scalable.

a hud must have an id.

a hud can be positioned either:
    - by anchoring one corner to the corner of another hud.
    - by anchoring the top left corner to an x y pixel position, relative to the top left of the screen
